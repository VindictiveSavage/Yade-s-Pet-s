ABOUT ME

(Pet Sitter/ Dog Walker)

Hi! Welcome to YADE'S PETS! I’m Jade, but my friends call me Yade. I've had animals my whole life, and have a passion for pets of all sizes and species. I turned my passion into an endeavor, which soon blossomed into Yade's Pets.


I am excited to have the privilege of caring for your pets; they are part of the family and will always be treated as such. Your babies will be cared for, loved, and played with just as if they were my own. (Anybody who knows me, knows how much I love my babies!) I look forward to meeting you and your pets and being able to provide personal care for your pets in the comfort of your home.
